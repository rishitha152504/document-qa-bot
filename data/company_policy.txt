Acme Corporation — Remote Work Policy (Effective January 2026)

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed 90 days of employment are eligible for hybrid or fully remote work arrangements.

2. Core Hours
Employees must be available between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM in their local time zone for meetings and collaboration.

3. Equipment
The company provides a laptop, monitor, and $500 home office stipend for eligible remote employees.

4. Security
All remote workers must use company VPN and enable multi-factor authentication on all corporate accounts.

5. Review
Remote work arrangements are reviewed annually by department managers.
